To find the billing- and usage-related UI, go to **[one.newrelic.com](https://one.newrelic.com)**, click the [user menu](/docs/accounts/accounts-billing/general-account-settings/intro-account-settings), then click **Administration**. You will land on the **Plan & usage** UI page, which shows an overview of your usage.

  title="New Relic usage UI"

/>
<figcaption>
  **[one.newrelic.com](https://one.newrelic.com) > User menu > Administration > Plan & usage:** The **Plan & usage** page gives details about your usage. From there you can get more details about your usage. 
</figcaption>
