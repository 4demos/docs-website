In the bottom left of the New Relic platform, you'll find your user menu, which gives you access to features related to managing your organization and users, managing your data, and more. 

## Find your account settings [#find]

From one.newrelic.com, go to the bottom left side of the platform, and click your name. This will open up the user menu options.
