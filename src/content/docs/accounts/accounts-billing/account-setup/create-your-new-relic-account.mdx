At New Relic, our mission is to help you create more perfect software. One way we help you is to give you **free access** to our observability platform, **forever**. 

No trial period. No need for a credit card until you're ready to unlock more New Relic power. 

We know you're intriqued.

* [Learn tips for optimizing your use of New Relic.](https://docs.newrelic.com/docs/using-new-relic/welcome-new-relic/get-started/introduction-new-relic#get-started-now)
