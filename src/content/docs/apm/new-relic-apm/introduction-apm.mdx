## How it all works

New Relic instruments your application at a code level through the use of one of our many language agents. These agents gather metrics from your application and send them to New Relic APM so you can monitor your app through pre-built dashboards. These dashboards allow you to monitor the following right out of the box:

- Response time of your application
- User experience through an [Apdex score](/docs/apm/new-relic-apm/apdex/apdex-measure-user-satisfaction)
- Throughput, or requests per minutes
- Error rate
